VIZAG

         

  • Visakhapatnam (Vizag) is a major port city on the eastern coast of India.


  • It is known for its beautiful beaches like RK Beach and Rushikonda Beach.


  • Vizag is home to the Eastern Naval Command of the Indian Navy.


  • The city is also famous for tourist spots like Araku Valley and the Kailasagiri Hill Park.
