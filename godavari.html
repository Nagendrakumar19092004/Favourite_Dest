GODAVARI

         

  • Rajahmundry is located on the banks of the Godavari River in Andhra Pradesh.


  • It is known for the Godavari Bridge, one of the longest rail-cum-road bridges in India.


  • The city is a major cultural and historical center of the Telugu-speaking region.


  • Rajahmundry is famous for its Pushkaram festival, held once every 12 years.
