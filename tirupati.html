TIRUPATI

         

  • Tirupati is home to the famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Andhra Pradesh.


  • The temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.


  • It is one of the wealthiest and most visited pilgrimage sites in the world..


  • The annual Brahmotsavam festival attracts millions of devotees to Tirupati.
