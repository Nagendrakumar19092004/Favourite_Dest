AGRA

         

  • Agra is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.


  • The city features the historic Agra Fort, another architectural marvel.


  • Agra is renowned for its rich Mughal history and heritage.


  • Famous for its sweet delicacy petha, Agra attracts tourists worldwide.
